Fernando & Hannah

 

 

Our Story

 

Destiny may sound cheesy

Yet that’s how it is exactly

Fernando and Hannah are meant to be!

 

Fernando and Hannah met in a way they never would have expected. Firstly, they are from the opposite sides of the world: Ecuador and the Philippines. Secondly, they were obviously raised in different cultures and language! And thirdly, by the time their story began, they were both doing pretty demanding postgraduate studies which leaves very little room for romance.

 

But indeed love works in mysterious ways. It was meant to be that as far as Melbourne in Australia, Fernando and Hannah will meet each other for the first time. And guess how? Not studying as you would expect from two PhD students, but dancing! One day they would try the same salsa class at the university, and the rest as they say is history…

 

Well, not really! It would have been too easy to say that they fell in love instantly. But it was only by talking and sharing activities with friends, that they will grow very fond of each other. They will stay hours talking without realising it. And the world seemed to stop between the two of them just like that.

 

But in any great story there must be a turning point, and theirs is not an exception. It happened when Hannah shared with Fernando her favourite book. It was a beautiful story about love and responsibility, and it was by reading each of the lines and Hannah’s annotations that Fernando knew she was the one. He prepared flowers, a guitar song from her favourite band Coldplay—all in a rooftop. He professed his love for her as the sun was setting down…it was the most romantic thing and yet only the birds against the orange-blue sky could have witnessed it.

 

Since then, they haven’t stopped going on adventures together! From something as simple as doing experiments together to traveling around the world to visit each other’s family.  But more importantly, making sure they feel loved with each passing day, despite their eventual long distance relationship as Hannah goes back to the Philippines for work. Yes, some days are too good to be true yet other times could be challenging as they get, but one thing is for sure:

 

Yes, indeed there is destiny

And as they accept love and responsibility

Fernando and Hannah will forever be!
