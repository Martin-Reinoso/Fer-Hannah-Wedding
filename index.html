Destiny may sound cheesy

Yet that’s how it is exactly

Fernando and Hannah are meant to be!

 

Fernando and Hannah met in a way they never would have expected. Firstly, they are from the opposite sides of the world: Ecuador and the Philippines. Secondly, they were obviously raised in different cultures and language! And thirdly, by the time their story began, they were both doing pretty demanding postgraduate studies which leaves very little room for romance.
